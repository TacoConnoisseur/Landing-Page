Hayao Miyazaki was born on January 5, 1941, and began his career as an animator in 1963.

As an entry level animator at Toei Animation, Asia's largest producers of animation, Hayao Miyazaki met his lifelong friend, collaborator, and business partner; Takahata Isao.

They continued to collaborate until their big hit film "Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind" in 1984, and together they launched their very own stuio in 1985 entitled, Studio Ghibli.
